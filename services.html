Servies

Westhart Deer Meats offers a wide range of services designed to meet the needs of every hunter. The company provides full deer processing, including skinning, cutting, grinding, and packaging, ensuring each customer receives clean, high-quality meat prepared to their specifications. In addition to traditional processing, Westhart Deer Meats creates specialty products such as sausage, jerky, and snack sticks for those who want to enjoy their harvest in different ways. For hunters interested in learning how to process their own deer, the company offers DIY Deer Processing Kits that include all the tools and instructions needed to complete the process at home. To further support learning, Westhart Deer Meats produces educational videos with step-by-step demonstrations that walk hunters through everything from field dressing to final packaging. The company also provides helpful advice on game handling, meat preservation, and equipment use, making it a one-stop shop for all deer processing and learning needs.

DIY Kits

Snack Stick Kit – Includes casings, seasoning blends, cure packets, and a step-by-step video tutorial.

Burger Blend Kit – Offers meat grinder attachments, mixing tools, and optional pork or beef fat packs for custom blends.

Sausage Making Kit – Comes with sausage casings, grinder and stuffer attachments, seasoning mixes, and a how-to guide.

Jerky Making Kit – Includes jerky gun, dehydrator racks, seasoning packets, curing salt, and detailed instructions.

Packaging and Storage Kit – Includes a vacuum sealer, bags, labels, and freezer-safe containers for organized storage.

Field Dressing Kit – Compact kit with field knives, gloves, wipes, zip ties, and a game bag for processing in the field.
