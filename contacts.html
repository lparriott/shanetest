Shane Westhart

founder of Westhart Deer Meats, grew up hunting and processing his own game from a young age. His passion for the outdoors and helping others led him to start a business that offers affordable deer processing and teaches hunters how to process their own meat. Shane takes pride in providing quality work, DIY kits, and educational videos that make the process simple and accessible for everyone.

Trey Stutheit

Trey Stutheit is the co-founder and business partner of Westhart Deer Meats, working alongside Shane Westhart. Trey and Shane grew up hunting together, sharing a passion for the outdoors and learning the skills of deer processing from a young age. Their shared experiences and dedication to the hunting community inspired them to start Westhart Deer Meats, a business focused on providing affordable deer processing, DIY kits, and educational resources for hunters. Trey brings hands-on expertise, creativity, and a commitment to helping others learn and enjoy the hunting tradition.
